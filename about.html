About Our Tea

Mystic Blue Spiced Tea is more than just a beverage; it’s a journey into tranquility and well-being. Our tea is crafted from the finest natural ingredients to provide a meditative experience with every cup.

Our unique blend includes Blue Lotus Flower, Butterfly Pea Flower, Licorice Root, St. John's Wort, Lavender, Hibiscus, Cinnamon, and Cayenne Pepper. Each ingredient is carefully selected for its health benefits and rich flavor profile.

Whether you are looking to relax after a long day or need a companion for your meditation sessions, Mystic Blue Spiced Tea is the perfect choice. Enjoy the soothing aroma and the calming effects of this exquisite blend.

Join our community of tea lovers who cherish the art of mindfulness and the pursuit of a balanced lifestyle. Discover the magic of Mystic Blue Spiced Tea today.
